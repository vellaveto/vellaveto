## Directive Format

Each directive should follow this format:

```
### Directive C-{number}: {title}
**Priority:** CRITICAL | HIGH | MEDIUM | LOW
**Affects:** Instance A | Instance B | Orchestrator | All
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

{Description of what to do and why, backed by research}

**Action Required:**
- [ ] Specific action item 1
- [ ] Specific action item 2
```
